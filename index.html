Who We Are

Global Leadership for Peace & Reconciliation, Inc. (Lead for Peace) is a non-profit organization dedicated to shaping leaders into strong advocates for peace and security. We aim to mitigate the cycle of violence by transforming the structures, policies, and practices that perpetuate it. potentials.

Core Principles and Values

Faithfulness

Fully committed to the growth of others.

Tenacity

Always seeking opportunities to grow.

Discipline

Making the right choices in every situation.

Excellence

Accepting pruning as a part of the process.

Perseverance

Realizing that growth takes time and respecting the process.

Resilience

Facing challenges with courage despite adversity.

Our Objectives

A World Without Violence.

Discover a unique approach to preventing violence through leadership transformation. At Lead for Peace, we believe in changing the world, one leader at a time.

Donate

Empower Leaders

Our brand strategy aims to empower religious, political, civic, and educational leaders to become catalysts for peace by providing them with the necessary resources, training, and support.

Donate

Forge Lasting Partnerships

We seek to establish meaningful and enduring partnerships with donors who share our vision for peace and reconciliation.

Donate

Promote Interfaith Understanding

Through our strategy, we aim to promote interfaith dialogue and understanding among religious leaders, fostering unity and harmony.

Donate

Advocate for Peace Policies

We strive to influence policies and practices at local, national, and international levels to create an environment conducive to peace.
