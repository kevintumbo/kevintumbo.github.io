Who We Are

At Global Leadership for Peace & Reconciliation, Inc. (Lead for Peace), we're redefining leadership for a more peaceful world.

As a dedicated non-profit, our mission is clear: to empower leaders to become champions of peace and security.

We tackle the root causes of violence by reshaping the systems, policies, and practices that sustain it.

By equipping leaders with the tools to drive meaningful change, we aim to create a future where every individual can thrive in safety and harmony.

At Lead for Peace, we believe that leadership isn't just about influence—it’s about building a world where peace is not the exception but the standard.

What We Do


At Lead for Peace, we're on a mission to create a world where peace, justice, and reconciliation are more than just ideals—they’re a reality.

Through impactful leadership and transformative initiatives, we empower individuals and communities to take action against the root causes of violence, injustice, and inequality. We are building a future where peace is not just a vision, but a lived experience
