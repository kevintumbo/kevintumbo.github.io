About Global Leadership for Peace & Reconciliation

Global Leadership for Peace & Reconciliation, Inc. (Lead for Peace)

is a non-profit organization dedicated to shaping leaders into strong advocates for peace and security.

We aim to mitigate the cycle of violence by transforming the structures, policies, and practices that perpetuate it.

Our mission is to equip leaders to become catalysts of peace and security by transforming structures, policies, and practices that drive violence, ensuring that all citizens can safely and peacefully achieve their full potential.

Prof. Abuelgasim Gour

Director, Global Human Rights Advocacy

Dr. Abuelgasim Gour is a professor of peace and conflict studies and the founder of the Institute of Studies and Culture of Peace at Sudan University of Science and Technology. He holds a Ph.D. in political philosophy and has published extensively on human rights, peace, philosophy, and African Indigenous Knowledge Systems.


With advanced studies in conflict resolution from CRI Seattle and postdoctoral research with the UN University for Peace, Dr. Gour has served as an academic advisor and commissioner for human rights. His work includes numerous publications on peace, drama, and political crises, alongside contributions to human rights reports.


Currently based in Cairo, Egypt, Dr. Gour continues his research and advocacy in peace and conflict studies.
