What We Do

At Lead for Peace, we're on a mission to create a world where peace, justice, and reconciliation are more than just ideals—they’re a reality.

Through impactful leadership and transformative initiatives, we empower individuals and communities to take action against the root causes of violence, injustice, and inequality. We are building a future where peace is not just a vision, but a lived experience.

At Lead for Peace, we are committed to cultivating a world of peace, justice, and reconciliation through strategic leadership and transformative action.

Our initiatives focus on empowering individuals and communities to address the root causes of violence, injustice, and inequality. Here’s how we make a difference

1. Establishing a Global Leadership Model for Peace

We are building a global leadership framework that unites leaders across sectors to collaborate on promoting peace and security. Through transformative actions and policies, we aim to create a world free from violence.

2. Training Leaders for Peace and Justice

Our leadership training programs equip individuals to act as agents of change. We empower leaders to challenge systems and structures that perpetuate violence, human rights violations, and environmental harm, fostering justice and reconciliation.

3. Capacity Building for Civic, Political, Religious, and Community Leaders

We offer educational opportunities that strengthen the leadership skills of civic, political, religious, and community leaders. Our programs focus on servant leadership, good governance, gender equality, economic empowerment, peace, and sustainable development.

4. Developing Educators for Peace and Social Justice

We train teachers to deliver educational programs that nurture character formation and leadership capabilities for peace, social justice, and reconciliation. These programs are designed for high schools, colleges, and universities at local, regional, national, and global levels.

5. Providing Healing Spaces for Grassroots and Religious Leaders

We create sacred spaces for grassroots and religious leaders to heal from trauma, enabling them to lead their communities and nations towards healing, reconciliation, and peace.

6. Global Leadership Forums and Summits

We organize summits, forums, and workshops that bring together religious, political, legal, cultural, and civil leaders. These events focus on fostering a shared understanding of leadership for reconciliation, inspiring collective action for peaceful coexistence and global harmony.

7. Fostering Interfaith and Inter-political Dialogue

We facilitate dialogues between diverse faith communities and political parties to build non-violent relationships, heal historical wounds, and repair past injustices, creating pathways for peaceful coexistence and communal healing.
